\n\r\n\r^bNow it is time to choose your character's ^BClass^?, which is the career-path that they will follow in the game.  This is your most important decision, choose wisely!^N

^XFighter :^? Fighters are brutish weapon masters, skilled in the art of killing, and invaluable in a close melee.

^XThief   :^? Thieves are skillful, sly, and devious, skilled at swiping, stealing, hiding, sneaking, trapping, poisoning, and more.

^XBard    :^? Bards are traveling singers, whose "songs" can possess powerful magic, who also qualify for many thief skills.

^XCleric  :^? Clerics are the great vassels of the Gods, who possess great magical abilities in their numerous "prayers".

^XMage    :^? Mages are masters of magic, and wielders of the great elemental powers.  

^XPaladin :^? Paladins are holy fighters, possessing many of the traits of both Fighters and Clerics.

^XDruid   :^? Druids are mystical guardians of the natural world, whose magic is drawn from earth, sea, and sky.

^XRanger  :^? Rangers are masters of the wild, possessing skills in combat, tracking, and some Druidic chants.
